# PMAAAI / PMAAA — Reference Notes for PMAFI Pages

The client (in their reply to the progress-update email) asked us to **"check
the website of PMAAAI for reference."** PMAAAI = Philippine Military Academy
Alumni Association, Inc. — a sister organization to PMAFI (the Foundation).
These notes capture what's useful as a model when building out PMAFI's pages.

## Sources
- **pmaaai.ph** — the official PMAAAI site. NOTE: threw TLS/certificate errors
  when fetched programmatically; open it directly in a browser.
- **pmaaa.org** — sister/related site, same org family, loaded fine and used for
  most of the structure below.
- Reviewed 2026-06-06.

## How the reference site is structured
Main navigation / sections observed:
- **The PMAAAI** — about / organization info
- **Membership** — registration, member types (e.g. Lifetime Member), amnesty program
- **Events & Programs** — events, *The Cavalier* magazine, e-newsletter, sports
- **Resources** — Legal Documents, Board Resolutions, Financial Documents
- **Tribute & Stories**
- **Chapters** — Army, Air Force, Navy, PNP, Coast Guard, Retirees/BIRC
- **Find & Connect** — community networking
- **Chairman's Message** on the homepage
- Motto: **"For God, Country, and the Corps!"**
- Leadership shown with officer photos + org structure (committee chairs,
  directors, ex-officio directors)

Contact (useful — likely confirms PMAFI's open "official address" question):

- Email: contact@pmaaa.org · Phone: TBA on their site

## What PMAFI could adopt (mapped to our pages / add-ons)
- **Chairman's / President's Message** — a short personal note from PMAFI
  leadership. New section (Home or About). Already in our Add-Ons list.
- **Motto / tagline** — surface a PMAFI tagline prominently, the way they use
  "For God, Country, and the Corps!"
- **Clear membership structure** — they show member types + a join flow.
  Validates PMAFI's membership system (Regular/Associate/Affiliate + apply +
  status check). Consider listing what each category gets.
- **Events & Programs / News** — AGMM (Annual General Membership Meeting),
  sports, magazine, newsletter → supports adding a **News/Events** section
  (already an Add-On).
- **Resources / transparency** — Board Resolutions, Financial Documents. Strong
  trust signal for a foundation that solicits donations; consider a simple
  "Reports / Transparency" page later.
- **Chapters** — PMAAAI organizes by service branch. Probably N/A for PMAFI,
  but worth noting the model exists.
